Miguel Neves

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at GéoAzur, Université Côte d’Azur where I am using observational seismology and deep learning to improve earthquake catalogs and study complex earthquake sequences.

Research interests:

  • Data mining and machine learning applications in seismology
  • Complex earthquake sequences
  • External stressors and seismicity
  • Slowly deforming regions
