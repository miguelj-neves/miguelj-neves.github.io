Miguel Neves

Observational Seismologist
University of Bergen, Norway

I am an observational seismologist working at the interface of seismic data analysis and machine learning. My research focuses on extracting high‑resolution information from continuous seismic data to improve earthquake catalogs, understand complex seismic sequences, and advance seismic monitoring, particularly in slowly deforming regions.

Research interests:

  • High‑resolution earthquake catalogs (machine‑learning and data‑driven methods)
  • Complex earthquake sequences
  • External stressors, anthropogenic activity, and seismicity
  • Real‑time and operational seismic monitoring
